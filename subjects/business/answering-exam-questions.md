# Answering Exam Questions

### Exams Words:

- Discuss
- Evaluate
- Role of
- Analyse
- Demonstrate
- Differentiate
- Identify
- Describe
- Explain

### Structure to Answer Questions:

SEE Method:

1. State
2. Explain
3. Example
