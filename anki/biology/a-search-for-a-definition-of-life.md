### A mnemonic device to learn all the characteristics of life

| Mnemonic                    | Word                                                         |
|-----------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------|
| One                         | Organisation                                                 |
| Nutty                       | Nutrition                                                    |
| Elephant                    | Excretion                                                    |
| Ran                         | Response                                                     |
| Riot                        | Reproduction                                                 |
| One nutty elephant ran riot | "Organisation, nutrition, excretion, response, reproduction" |
